The Bake Boss Story

From Our First Mixer to Multi-City Outlets.

Bake Boss started with a simple idea: bringing the comforting warmth of a local bakery together with the exciting flavors of quick Indian fast food. We realized that whether you crave a decadent Red Velvet or a spicy Paneer Pizza, the expectation is the same: speed and sensational taste.

Driven by local passion and commitment to quality, we've expanded to multiple locations, becoming the go-to spot for those who want the best of both worlds—sweet and savory—without the long wait. Every bite from Bake Boss is a testament to our journey and our community.
